What Makes Us Mumbai’s Best Residential Interior Designers?


We at Thar Furniture, think that houses should be filled with things you like seeing while still being useful to fit your lifestyle, which is why your needs and personal style are excellent guides for us.


Our interior designers are sure to have a perfect fit for every interior style and budget, whether you are looking for a complete one-stop solution provider for interior design, decoration of your new house, re-modeling, and renovation of your existing home, or even just a part of your house that you want a new look for.


Indeed, as interior designers with an eye for beauty, we frequently come across homes that were created solely out of love and appreciation for specific interior designs, structures, colors, and patterns, and these homes always appear and remain beautiful regardless of time and history.


While the interior design for different areas of your home, such as the living room, dining room, kitchen, bedroom, bathrooms, entryway, children’s room, balcony, and so on, may have different designs and aesthetics, we will make sure they all work together nicely. Our ability to achieve just that for each and every one of our clients is what distinguishes us as one of Mumbai’s top home interior designers.
